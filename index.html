About the Contest

"Sahar Ka Sher" competition celebrates the best and most attractive shops in the city. Shops with the highest votes will be crowned as winners.

Best Shop Competition — Overview

Below are the key points about the Best Shop Competition.

  1. The Best Shop Competition is being organized to find the most outstanding shop in the area.
  2. Every shop can participate by submitting its nomination form.
  3. The competition aims to promote quality service and customer satisfaction.
  4. Shops will be judged based on public voting and overall presentation.
  5. The shop with the highest number of votes will be declared the winner.
  6. This event encourages healthy competition among local businesses.
  7. It also helps shop owners improve their services and build a better reputation.
  8. Customers play a major role by supporting and voting for their favorite shop.
  9. Voting guidelines must be followed to ensure fairness in the competition.
  10. The winner will be honored with the title “Best Shop of the Year” and receive public recognition.

Rules & Regulations

  • Only registered shops can participate.
  • Voting must be fair and genuine.
  • Multiple fake votes may lead to disqualification.
  • Organizers hold the right to final decisions.

Voting Guidelines

Voting Guidelines

  1. All voters must provide valid details before submitting a vote.
  2. Each person is allowed to vote only once in the competition.
  3. Votes must be cast for genuine shops participating in the contest.
  4. No duplicate, fake, or automated votes will be accepted.
  5. Voters should review shop details before making a final choice.
  6. Misleading or promoting false information during voting is prohibited.
  7. Voting will remain open only during the official contest time.
  8. Any attempt to manipulate or cheat the voting system may lead to disqualification.
  9. Voters must follow all rules to ensure a fair and transparent competition.
  10. The decision of the organizing committee will be final based on valid votes.
